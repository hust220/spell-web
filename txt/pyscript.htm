Download this script and do the following to visualize using PyMOL.

  1. Open PyMOL (v.0.99 or higher recommended).
  2. Type the following and hit enter:
    run <script-file>

Note: Independent objects representing the protein before and after minimization will be generated automatically. PyMOL CGO objects with clashes rendered as sticks are generated for the protein before and after minimization. The entire range of VDW energies for all the clashes is displayed as a color bar.
